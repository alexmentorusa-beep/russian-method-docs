Practice Assignment
How to Work With This Lesson

Follow the steps below in order · No memorization required

This lesson is designed for independent self-study at any time convenient for you. There is no need to memorize anything. Your goal is to experience the language — not to study it. Follow the steps below in order.
1
Listen Once — Just Listen
Press play and listen to the full recording without doing anything else. Don't look at the text yet. Let the sounds of Russian wash over you. Notice the rhythm, the melody, the feeling of the words.
2
Read and Visualize
Switch to English mode and read the text without pronouncing the words — and try to visualize the story given. See the people, the situation, the emotion. Make it real in your mind.
3
⭐ Simultaneous Repetition — The Core Exercise
This is the heart of the method. Switch to Russian only mode. Press play and repeat out loud simultaneously with the narrator, staying 1–2 words behind. Follow the Russian text with your eyes. Do not pause the recording.

If you miss words — that is completely normal at first. With each repetition you will catch more and more. Repeat the full recording as many times as necessary until it feels natural and effortless. 💡 Engage your senses as you repeat — picture Oleg and Maria, feel the warmth of the word любовь, let yourself smile. The more you feel, the deeper the language takes root.
4
Eyes Closed — Trust Yourself
Do a few more repetitions with your eyes closed. Don't worry about perfection. Let the phrases come as they come. This is where the language begins its journey from your conscious mind to your subconscious speech center.
5
Final Check
Switch to English only mode. Read each English phrase and try to say it in Russian out loud — without looking at the Russian text. If a phrase comes naturally, without effort — it is yours. If not, go back to Step 3 for those phrases.
Remember: You are not trying to memorize. You are training your brain to respond automatically — exactly the way you learned your own language as a child.
